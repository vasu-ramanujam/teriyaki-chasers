## Summary

**Mock Data** = **Fake, hardcoded responses** that look real but don't actually identify anything. It's like a "placeholder" that keeps your app working when real AI isn't available!

- **Real AI**: Actually analyzes images with neural networks
- **Mock Data**: Just returns the same fake results every time
- **Use Case**: Development, testing, and emergency fallback
